## Risk Management Register Rules

When creating risk registers or risk assessments:

**Format requirements:**
- Use vertical card format (NOT wide tables)
- Do NOT use triple backticks
- Group by risk category or priority
- Include risk scoring

**Risk Register Format:**

### Risk Register

#### High Priority Risks

**RISK-001: [Risk Title]**
- **Category:** Technical / Operational / Financial / Strategic
- **Description:** Clear description of the risk
- **Likelihood:** High (4) / Medium (3) / Low (2) / Very Low (1)
- **Impact:** Critical (4) / Major (3) / Moderate (2) / Minor (1)
- **Risk Score:** [Likelihood × Impact] = X
- **Current Controls:** Existing measures in place
- **Mitigation Strategy:** Planned risk response
- **Owner:** Responsible person/role
- **Review Date:** Next review date

**RISK-002: [Risk Title]**
- **Category:** Category type
- **Description:** Risk description
- **Likelihood:** Medium (3)
- **Impact:** Major (3)
- **Risk Score:** 9
- **Current Controls:** What's in place
- **Mitigation Strategy:** Response plan
- **Owner:** Responsible party
- **Review Date:** Date

#### Medium Priority Risks

**RISK-003: [Risk Title]**
- **Category:** Category
- **Description:** Description
- **Likelihood:** Medium (3)
- **Impact:** Moderate (2)
- **Risk Score:** 6
- **Current Controls:** Controls
- **Mitigation Strategy:** Strategy
- **Owner:** Owner
- **Review Date:** Date

**Risk Matrix Reference:**
    Impact →   Minor  Moderate  Major  Critical
    Likelihood     (1)     (2)    (3)      (4)
    ↓
    High (4)        4       8     12       16
    Medium (3)      3       6      9       12
    Low (2)         2       4      6        8
    Very Low (1)    1       2      3        4

    Scores: 1-4 Low | 5-9 Medium | 10-16 High

**Rules:**
- Calculate risk score (Likelihood × Impact)
- Group by priority (High > Medium > Low)
- Include current controls AND mitigation
- Assign clear ownership
- Keep descriptions actionable
- Maximum 5-6 risks per priority group initially

**For comprehensive registers:**
- Offer to focus on specific risk categories
- Offer to expand High priority risks in detail
