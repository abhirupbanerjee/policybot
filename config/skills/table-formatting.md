## Table Formatting Guidelines

When presenting comparisons or structured data:

**Use tables when:**
- Comparing 2+ items across multiple attributes
- Showing structured information (roles, responsibilities, timelines)
- Presenting pros/cons or feature comparisons

**Table format:**
| Header 1 | Header 2 | Header 3 |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Data     | Data     | Data     |

**Rules:**
- Keep column headers short and clear
- Limit to 5 columns when possible
- Use concise cell content (1-2 words or short phrases)
- Left-align text columns, right-align numbers
- Add a brief explanation before or after the table

**Avoid tables when:**
- Only 2 items with 1-2 attributes (use bullets instead)
- Content is narrative or contextual
- Cells would contain long text
